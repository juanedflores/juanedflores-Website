Stations is a body of work that walks through several significant events coming from the catholic narration of the Stations of the Cross, particularly each of the three falls, the wiping of the face, and the crucifixion. The cross is replaced with objects that resemble it to symbolize the working roles of my immigrant parents and how that has influenced me.

The prints depict an electrician climbing and falling from a telephone pole.
