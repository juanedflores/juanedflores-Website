Toy Instrument Compositions (2020)

The Toy Instrument Compositions are a series of experiments using found electronic toys that resemble musical instruments. Each of the compositions utilize some sort of circuit bending to create an infinite loop of a sound which is turned on and off periodically by a relay controlled by a Raspberry Pi.

Site: Brick at Blue Star. San Antonio, TX.

Materials: Found electronics, conductive tape, Raspberry Pi, relays.
