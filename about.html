Bio

Juan is a multidisciplinary artist born in Del Rio, TX and living in the southeast side of San Antonio, TX. He received his BA in Art from Connecticut College in New London, CT. His start was in the Blue Star Contemporary Art Museum's MOSAIC program.

Artist Statement

I work in a variety of traditional medias but currently I am fascinated by algorithmic, generative, and interactive art using free, accessible, and emerging technologies. This includes exploring digital spaces, artificial intelligence, robotics, machine learning, virtual and augmented reality, and creative coding.
