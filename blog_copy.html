  • About This Blog

    I created this blog in 2021 to keep track of my learning process in various topics. It has a focus in Art & Technology. While it started as a reference for my future self, I try to write in a way that may be accessible for someone to follow along. I am interested in blogging as a " digital garden ".

    A more detailed explanation of the functionality of this blog is documented in the 'More About This Blog' post. Which is, like many entries in this blog, always a work in progress.
